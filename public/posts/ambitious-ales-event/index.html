Ambitious Ales Event

Mon Jan 17, 2022

A most excellent event, Ambitious Ales Indie Game Night put on by Max and OC Drawing Royale!

We had an awesome time playing games like “Rookie of the Year” by Two Rabbit Games and Hexachessagon by Spherous Games just to name a couple. Watching folks play Realm Uniter and getting our first feedback was so valuable. Thank you! The artists who drew pieces inspired by the handful of games did incredible work! Check out this awesome sketch by an artist at the event. We dig the Adventure Time vibes.

We are so grateful for the community and new friends made. We will most definitely be at the next one. See you there!
