Realm Uniter

An action-adventure game

Realm Uniter is a top-down action-adventure game featuring souls-lite combat with rpg and metroidvania elements. Play as an adventurer who stumbles upon the land of Asmuldan. Once a harmonius kingdom, Asmuldan has fallen into disarray. In a land divided by hatred, misunderstanding and greed, do you have what it takes to unite the realm?

Adventure through a fantasy world

Explore forests, caves, towns and more, all rendered in colorful, retro-inspired pixel art. Talk to a variety of characters and creatures, find secret areas, shortcuts and hidden items, and uncover a plot that only you can solve.

Fight a Variety of Enemies

Rely on your reflexes as you dodge, block and attack your way through a diverse set of enemies intent to stop you in your task. Learn the timing of your enemies attack patterns and exploit them.

Find a Vast Arsenal of Weapons, Items and Armor

Acquire and equip a variety of weapons, armor and items to customize your playstyle. No classes or restrictions means you can customize your build however you want. Build into classic archetypes like a heavy-armored knight, light and nimble spellcaster, or mix and match and create your own build!
